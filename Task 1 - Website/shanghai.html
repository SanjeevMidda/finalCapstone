Shanghai

Shanghai is the largest city in China and is located on the eastern coast of the country. With a population of over 24 million people, it is one of the most populous cities in the world.

Shanghai is also a popular tourist destination, known for its modern skyscrapers, historic landmarks, and cultural attractions. Some of the most popular tourist attractions in Shanghai include the Shanghai Tower, the Shanghai World Financial Center, and the Oriental Pearl Tower. The city is also home to many museums, parks, and gardens, as well as a vibrant nightlife scene.
