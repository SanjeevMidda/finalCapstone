SEOUL

Seoul is the capital and largest city of South Korea. It is located on the Han River in the northwest of the country, and has a population of over 10 million people.

Seoul is known for its modern and vibrant city life, and it is a major cultural, economic, and political center. The city is home to many iconic landmarks and tourist attractions, such as the Gyeongbokgung Palace, the Namsan Tower, and the Myeong-dong shopping district.
