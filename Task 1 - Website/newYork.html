New York

New York City is the largest city in the United States and is located in the state of New York. It is known as the "City That Never Sleeps" due to its vibrant and fast-paced city life.

New York City is a major cultural and financial center, and it is home to many iconic landmarks and tourist attractions. Some of the most famous landmarks in New York City include the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and the Empire State Building. The city is also home to many world-class museums, such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Museum of Modern Art.
